FAQ

Quick answers before you email.

How fast do you respond?

We try to respond as quickly as possible, especially for bug reports and billing questions.

Can I send feedback even if I am not a paying user?

Yes. Early feedback from free users is useful because it shows where Pact needs to be clearer.

Where do I manage subscriptions or trials?

Trials and subscriptions are managed in your Apple App Store account settings.

Should I include private information?

No. Please do not send passwords, payment details, or anything you would not want handled by email.
